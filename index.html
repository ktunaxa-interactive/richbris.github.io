About me

As a Seattle artist, having grown up in Tacoma and Spokane, I feel a strong conneections to the lifestyles of the Pacific Northwest. My collages reflect the diversity of styles that I find. My graffiti projects are concerned with reclaiming the cultural space via art, re-tagging random public graffiti with the meaningful Native American elements of my ancestors and the Salish traditions of the Puget Sound area.

Please contact me for the pricelist, commissions, or to say hi.
